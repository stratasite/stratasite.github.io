## Where MetricFlow is strong

MetricFlow is the closest peer on the semantic side. It links entities by name, aggregates then joins
across models, lives in version control, and fits a dbt centric stack naturally.

## Where Strata pulls ahead

MetricFlow asks for per-model YAML, is headless with no dashboards, and is bound to a single warehouse.
Strata blends automatically and grain-safely by naming, ships five measure types, routes across
federated hot tiers for sub-second answers, and is a beautiful self-service layer in its own right. The
edge is the whole package working together, not any single feature.
