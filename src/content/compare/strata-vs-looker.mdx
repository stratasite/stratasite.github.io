## Where Looker is strong

Looker is the closest full-stack analogue: a mature semantic layer plus BI, with
strong security and a self-contained experience.

## Where Strata differs

Looker carries LookML configuration fatigue, awkward multi-fact handling (merged
results), weak LOD/snapshot expressiveness, no multi-engine hot-tier federation,
and a "configure everything" design philosophy. Strata blends automatically by
convention, federates across engines with partition-aware hot-tier routing, ships
five measure types, and is beautiful by default.
