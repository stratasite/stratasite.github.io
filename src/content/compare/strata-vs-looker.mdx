## Where Looker is strong

Looker is the closest full-stack analogue: a mature semantic layer plus BI, with strong security and a
self-contained experience a team can standardize on.

## Where Strata pulls ahead

Looker makes you configure every explore in LookML, handles multiple facts awkwardly through merged
results, and has weak LOD and snapshot expressiveness, with no multi-engine hot-tier federation. Strata
blends automatically by naming, stays grain-safe by construction, federates across engines for
sub-second answers, ships five measure types, and is beautiful by default. You trade a
configure-everything grind for convention.
