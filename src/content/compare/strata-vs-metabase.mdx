## Where Metabase is strong

Metabase is beloved for how fast a team gets from a connected database to a first dashboard. The
question builder is friendly, self-service is genuinely easy, and it is open-source and cost-effective
to run.

## Where Strata pulls ahead

Metabase gives you only a thin semantic layer, so blending and correctness are on you, and there is no
federation or hot-tier routing. Measure expressiveness stays basic, and its AI is a bolt-on rather than
grain-safe retrieval. Strata keeps the easy self-service but puts a governed grain-safe semantic layer
underneath, with automatic blending by naming, five measure types, aggregate-aware federated
performance, and agent-safe retrieval. It augments or replaces Metabase rather than connecting to it.
