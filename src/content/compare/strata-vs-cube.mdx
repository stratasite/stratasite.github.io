## Where Cube Is Strong

Cube has strong managed pre-aggregations and caching, a broad API and BI ecosystem, and it is a common
choice for embedded analytics.

## Where Strata Pulls Ahead

Cube is headless with no native dashboards, and it carries a heavy configuration burden of cubes and
views with weak LOD and snapshot expressiveness. Strata is aggregate-aware and federates across engines
with partition-aware routing, models by naming instead of authored cubes, ships five measure types, and
gives you a beautiful consumption layer for people and agents.
