## Where Snowflake Is Strong

Snowflake semantic views are native to where your data already lives, inherit strong RLS and masking,
are agent-native through Cortex, and ship AI-assisted modeling.

## Where Strata Pulls Ahead

> In Snowflake, two dimensions with the same name from different tables break the semantic view. In
> Strata, that same shared name is the signal that they are conformed and safely blendable.

Snowflake is Snowflake-only, with no hot-tier federation and no built-in self-service or dashboards.
Strata fronts your warehouse with a fast OLAP hot tier and aggregate awareness, blends across domains by
convention, and is itself the consumption layer for humans and agents.
