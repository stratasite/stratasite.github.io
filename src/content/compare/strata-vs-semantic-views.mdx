## Where Snowflake is strong

Snowflake semantic views are native (already where the data is), inherit strong
RLS/masking, are deeply agent-native (Cortex), and ship AI-assisted modeling.

## The structural difference

> In Snowflake, two dimensions with the same name from different tables **break**
> the semantic view. In Strata, that same shared name **is** the signal that
> they're conformed and safely blendable.

Snowflake is Snowflake-only (no hot-tier federation) with no built-in
self-service or dashboards. Strata fronts the warehouse with a fast OLAP hot tier
and aggregate awareness, and is itself the consumption layer.
