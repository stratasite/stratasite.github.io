## Where Tableau is strong

Tableau is best in class for visualization and visual exploration, has a huge ecosystem, and pioneered
LOD expressions like FIXED and EXCLUDE.

## Where Strata pulls ahead

Tableau has no governed semantic layer and no multi-engine federation, so correctness and performance
fall on you while extracts and per-workbook config pile up. Strata is the governed grain-safe layer
underneath, with the LOD idea lifted into reusable governed measures, federated hot-tier performance,
and agent-safe retrieval. It augments or replaces Tableau rather than connecting to it.
