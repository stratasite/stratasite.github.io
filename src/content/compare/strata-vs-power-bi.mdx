## Where Power BI is strong

Power BI is ubiquitous and cost-effective, with deep Microsoft integration, the DAX language, and
strong reach into warehouses like Snowflake.

## Where Strata pulls ahead

Power BI modeling and DAX grow complex fast, import and refresh performance is tied to the model, and
governance fragments across workspaces, none of it built for agents. Strata is one governed, grain-safe
semantic layer across engines, with federated hot-tier performance, five measure types, agent-safe
retrieval, and beautiful self-service. It augments or replaces Power BI rather than feeding it.
