## Who Is the Primary Operator?

The primary operator is the person who can drive the full tool, not just consume it. In Power BI that
person is deeply technical: they master a complex UI, understand the data model, know some SQL, and
usually learn DAX. Non-technical users stay passive, limited to the slicers a report author enabled. In
Strata the primary operator is the non-technical user. They build reports, dashboards, agents, and
exports themselves; engineers set up and lightly maintain.

## Where Power BI Is Strong

Power BI is genuinely powerful. Signup is frictionless, the connector list is vast,
and data prep, semantic modeling, and report building all live in one desktop flow. Every source, even a
CSV, becomes a semantic model, and DAX can express almost any measure. For a Microsoft shop with
technical authors, it is a strong end-to-end tool.

## Where Strata Pulls Ahead

**DAX is the tax.** Power BI's expressiveness runs through DAX, which does not map cleanly to SQL and
takes real time to master. Even a semi-additive closing balance needs a CALCULATE and ENDOFMONTH
formula. Strata ships five measure types plus one-click YoY, moving average, and percent of total, with
no new language to learn.

**The model trusts you.** Put Store Sales and Web Sales in one model and Power BI will happily let you
slice Web Sales by a Store dimension via **cross join**. Correctness rests on the author knowing every
legal combination. Strata derives what is legal and refuses invalid combinations before they run, so
blends stay grain-safe.

**Models proliferate, outside Git.** Every use case tends to spawn another semantic model, and
versioning is built in but not Git. Dozens accumulate with no shared source of truth. Strata models a
whole domain comprehensively in a single semantic layer, all version controlled via Git.

**The rest adds up.** Import, DirectQuery, and Composite are all fast, but lock you further into the
Microsoft ecosystem. Strata federates with aggregate-aware, partition-aware hot-tier routing across
popular OLAP and warehouse engines. Dashboard layout is pixel-perfect and manual, as pretty or as ugly
as the author makes it. Strata is beautiful by default and agent-safe out of the box. It augments or
replaces Power BI for generalized self-service needs.
