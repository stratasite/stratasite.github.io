## Where Superset Is Strong

Superset, and Preset in its hosted form, is open-source and SQL-native, flexible, and cost-effective to
self-host.

## Where Strata Pulls Ahead

Superset has only a thin semantic layer, so blending and correctness are on you, and there is no
federation or hot-tier routing. Output takes real effort to keep consistent, and none of it is built
for agents. Strata adds a governed grain-safe semantic layer, automatic blending by naming,
aggregate-aware federated performance, and beautiful self-service by default, all agent-safe. It
augments or replaces Superset rather than connecting to it.
