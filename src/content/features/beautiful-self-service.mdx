## Convention over Configuration

Strata's dashboarding philosophy is *beautiful by default*. A few sensible conventions do the work
that manual configuration usually demands. Drop your views in the order you want them read and
Strata arranges them into a polished, professional layout automatically. It reads each view's
visualization type and width mode to size and place it. No dragging, snapping, or nudging.

The default palette is considered and accessible. It works out of the box in light and dark mode,
so every chart stays legible without a color picker in sight. Stop fighting unwieldy grids, control
placement, and sizing. Embrace the conventions and get a great dashboard on the first try.

## Interactive Dashboards

Go from a static report to a fully interactive dashboard in a few clicks. Add the controls you want,
like filters, date ranges, and category pickers, and Strata places them for you. No deciding where a
control belongs. No broken layout when you add one more. No overflow to clean up. The controls drive
every view on the report, so anyone who opens it can explore on their own.

Interactive filters are also personalized, so a dashboard you share works for your whole team.
Each colleague's filter settings are saved and reapplied on their next visit.

## Thoughtful Integration of AI for Productivity

Strata has a conversational interface for agentic use cases, but we still believe humans deserve a
powerful editor. AI is sprinkled thoughtfully throughout that editor with a single goal: making the
person building the dashboard faster.

The analogy is the IDE. Even engineers who lean heavily on AI still review and refine the generated
code in a real editor. Strata gives non-technical analysts the same leverage. AI does the heavy
lifting, and the human stays in control of the result.

A few of the places AI shows up:

1. **AI relevance search** across your fields and measures
2. **View building by drag and drop or prompt**, with completions that assemble the output for you
3. **Filters composed from a plain language prompt**
4. **Calculations authored from an AI prompt**
5. **Conversational creation of custom visualization types**
