## The five types

Standard (simple aggregations), Complex (formulas over other measures), Snapshot
(semi-additive, point-in-time), LOD (include/exclude dimensions for share, % of
total, fixed denominators), and ad-hoc measures and dimensions users derive on
the fly through the calculation creator.

## One-click transforms

From any measure, generate year-over-year versions, moving averages, and
percent-to-total calculations in a click — no engineering required.
